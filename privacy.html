Privacy Policy

Kanshu Reader (看書) · Effective March 15, 2026

Summary

Kanshu does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your reading data stays entirely on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No account creation or sign-in
  • No cookies or fingerprinting

Data Stored on Your Device

All app data — your imported books, saved words, reading progress, bookmarks, highlights, and coach profile — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is never sent to any server.

Optional Cloud AI Features

Kanshu offers optional grammar and translation features powered by third-party AI services (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, Google Gemini). These features are disabled by default and require you to provide your own API key in Settings.

When you use these features, the text of the sentence you're reading is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you selected. Kanshu does not route this data through any intermediary server. Your use of these services is governed by the respective provider's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

Kanshu does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Kanshu does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
