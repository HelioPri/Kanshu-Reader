FAQ

  • What Chinese level do I need? Kanshu works for all levels — from HSK 1 beginners to advanced readers. Set your HSK level during onboarding and the coach adapts to you.
  • Does it work offline? Yes. The core reader, dictionary, and grammar notes all work without an internet connection. Cloud AI features are optional.
  • What AI features are available? On-device AI (Apple Intelligence, iOS 26+) and cloud providers (Claude, OpenAI, Gemini) for contextual grammar explanations. All are optional enhancements.
  • What file formats can I import? EPUB, PDF, plain text (.txt), and web URLs with automatic encoding detection for Chinese sites.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Kanshu ("the App") is developed by Priyesh. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data stored on your device. All reading data — books, saved words, bookmarks, highlights, reading progress, coaching data, and flashcard schedules — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to any server.

Optional cloud AI features. If you choose to enable cloud AI providers (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or Google Gemini), text snippets from your current reading may be sent to those providers to generate grammar explanations. This only happens when you explicitly request it (e.g., tapping a word with cloud AI enabled). API keys for these services are stored locally on your device and are never shared with us.

Apple services. The App may use Apple's on-device translation and intelligence features (iOS 17.4+ / iOS 26+). These are processed on-device or by Apple according to Apple's own privacy policies.

No accounts or analytics. Kanshu does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and does not include any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

No data sharing. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Changes to this policy. If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact. For privacy-related questions, email patelpriyesh@hotmail.com.
