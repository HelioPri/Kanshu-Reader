How It Works

From import to mastery in four steps

1

Import a Book

Load an EPUB, PDF, text file, or paste a web URL. Kanshu segments the Chinese text into tappable words automatically.

2

Read with Tap-to-Learn

Read naturally, tapping any word you don't know for instant pinyin, definitions, and grammar notes. Toggle pinyin mode for full annotations.

3

Coach Detects Patterns

The coach silently observes your lookups, translations, and reading speed. When it spots a grammar pattern you're struggling with, it serves a targeted mini-challenge.

4

Review & Retain

Saved words enter the SM-2 spaced repetition queue. Review a few cards daily to move vocabulary from short-term to long-term memory.

FAQ

  • What Chinese level do I need? Kanshu works for all levels, from HSK 1 beginners to advanced readers. Set your HSK level during onboarding and the coach adapts to you. Your initial choice seeds the pattern confidence model, but it recalibrates quickly based on your actual reading behavior.
  • Does it work offline? Yes. The core reader, 120,000-entry dictionary, and 20,000+ pre-generated grammar notes all work without an internet connection. On-device Apple Intelligence (iOS 26+) also works offline. Cloud AI features are a powerful optional add-on.
  • What AI features are available? Three tiers: bundled grammar notes (always available), on-device Apple Intelligence (iOS 26+, no data leaves your device), and cloud AI providers (Claude, OpenAI, Gemini) for rich contextual explanations. The app tries each tier in order and falls back automatically.
  • What file formats can I import? EPUB (with full chapter structure preserved), PDF (text extraction via PDFKit), plain text (.txt) with auto-detected chapter headings, and web URLs with GB18030 encoding detection for Chinese sites.
  • How does the grammar coaching work? The coach silently observes your first 8 pages, tracking which words you tap, which grammar you look up, and how long you spend on each page. It maintains a registry of 100+ grammar patterns organized into tiers (foundational, intermediate, advanced) and uses Bayesian confidence scoring to detect your weak areas. When it spots a pattern you're struggling with, it generates a targeted mini-challenge. It never interrupts more than 3 times per session.
  • Can I use my own AI API key? Yes. Go to Settings, then Cloud AI, and enter your API key for Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or Google Gemini. Kanshu also supports any OpenAI-compatible endpoint, so you can point it at a self-hosted model. Your API keys are stored locally on your device and never sent to us.
  • What's the difference between the segmentation engines? Kanshu offers three engines. CC-CEDICT uses a 120k-entry dictionary with bidirectional maximum matching — best for complete dictionary info on every word. Apple NL uses Apple's NLTokenizer for natural word boundaries that match how native speakers read. Jieba uses statistical HMM segmentation — best for texts with slang, internet language, or neologisms.
  • Can I export my word list? Yes. You can export your saved words in Pleco format, which works with Pleco and other Chinese learning apps. Access the export option from your word list in the library.
  • Does it support traditional Chinese? The dictionary and reader handle both simplified and traditional characters. The CC-CEDICT dictionary includes traditional character entries. Segmentation works with both character sets.
  • What devices and iOS versions are supported? Kanshu requires iOS 17.4 or later. On-device AI features (Apple Intelligence) require iOS 26+ and supported hardware. The app runs on iPhone and iPad.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Kanshu ("the App") is developed by Priyesh. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data stored on your device. All reading data — books, saved words, bookmarks, highlights, reading progress, coaching data, and flashcard schedules — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to any server.

Optional cloud AI features. If you choose to enable cloud AI providers (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or Google Gemini), text snippets from your current reading may be sent to those providers to generate grammar explanations. This only happens when you explicitly request it (e.g., tapping a word with cloud AI enabled). API keys for these services are stored locally on your device and are never shared with us.

Apple services. The App may use Apple's on-device translation and intelligence features (iOS 17.4+ / iOS 26+). These are processed on-device or by Apple according to Apple's own privacy policies.

No accounts or analytics. Kanshu does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and does not include any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

No data sharing. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Changes to this policy. If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact. For privacy-related questions, email patelpriyesh@hotmail.com.
